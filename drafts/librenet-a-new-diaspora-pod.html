Hello world! A new Diaspora* pod has come to life. It was initially deployed for the Greek users, but the registrations are open so anyone can sign up for a new account and connect to the Diaspora network .

Visit https://librenet.gr now!

At this time of writing we host something less than 300 users. And that has happened in less in a month!
